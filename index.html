Current Doctoral Research

Title: AI Integration in Kaizen Continuous Improvement: Leadership Strategies for Operational Excellence in U.S. Property & Casualty Insurance

Overview: As part of my doctoral program at the Swiss School of Business and Management, I am conducting original research on how U.S.-based Property & Casualty (P&C) insurance companies integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI) into their operations while applying Kaizen continuous improvement principles.

This study examines the role of leadership strategy, organizational culture, and change management in successfully embedding AI into processes to enhance operational excellence. It bridges academic insight with practical application, informed by my professional experience leading enterprise technology transformations.

Why It Matters: The findings aim to offer a framework that not only benefits insurance companies but also serves as a model for other industries exploring AI adoption within a continuous improvement context.
